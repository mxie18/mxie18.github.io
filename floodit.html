Flood-It!

To reiterate, the objective in Flood-It! is to fill the board with any given color within a move limit. The flood always starts at the top left, and we can denote the top left cell color to be the current flood color. As you click new squares, the flood color changes to the color of that new square and the board "floods" from the top left. The flooding works by coloring the top left blob (a group of neighboring cells all with the same color) of cells with the new color.
